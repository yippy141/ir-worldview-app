# Results Page Spec - IR Worldview Inventory

## Goal
Replace the current "label + bars" output with a results experience that explains meaning, tensions, and limitations.

## Section order
1. Hero
2. Plain-English summary
3. What most drove your result
4. Where you are mixed
5. Dimension profile
6. Closest neighboring worldview
7. Glossary
8. Suggested reading
9. Methods / limitations note

## 1. Hero
- Result label
- One-sentence subtitle
- Small note: "Prototype classification, not a validated psychometric diagnosis"

## 2. Plain-English summary
Template:
"You tend to see world politics primarily through [top family logic], but your answers also show strong [secondary logic]. In practice, you seem most persuaded by arguments about [top dimension 1], [top dimension 2], and [top scenario tendency]."

## 3. What most drove your result
Show 3 cards:
- Strongest dimension
- Second-strongest dimension
- Most revealing scenario choice

Example card labels:
- "Institutions, but conditionally"
- "Political economy is central"
- "Strategic sectors trigger harder-edged instincts"

## 4. Where you are mixed
Generate 2-3 tensions such as:
- high institutions + high security competition
- high order + high human-rights override
- high political economy + low postcolonial skepticism

Use copy like:
"You are not a pure institutionalist. You become more competitive when the issue turns strategic."

## 5. Dimension profile
Keep 1-7 bars for actual measured dimensions.
Do not label them as percentiles.
Add one-line explanation under each bar.

Example:
- Institutional efficacy: "You think institutions can matter independently, but only when they are credible and not obviously captured."

## 6. Closest neighboring worldview
Show:
- Primary family
- Runner-up family
- Why the overlap exists

## 7. Glossary
Inline definitions for:
- realism
- institutionalism
- constructivism
- political economy
- pluralism
- solidarism
- restraint

## 8. Suggested reading
Give 3-5 short items based on the result family.

## 9. Methods / limitations note
Short box:
- prototype
- branching logic is heuristic
- scores are comparative within this model
- not a measure of knowledge or expertise

## Important implementation note
The current "profile clarity" and "family fit" values are internal heuristic outputs.
They should not be displayed as if they are validated confidence scores or population percentiles.
