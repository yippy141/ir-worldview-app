# V13 what not to do

## Do not add scope
- Do not add new IR modules.
- Do not add new scored AI archetypes.
- Do not add accounts.
- Do not build a dashboard.
- Do not start a full psychometric validation project.
- Do not spin AI into a separate app yet.

## Do not over-design Profile
- No constellation/node map.
- No radar chart.
- No decorative emblem that carries no meaning.
- No more than one dominant visual above the fold.
- No repeated stable/shift/tension blocks.

## Do not overclaim
- Do not say the tool discovers a true type.
- Do not imply the module overlays are validated measurements.
- Do not display AI clarity as a scientific confidence score.
- Do not call pseudonymous raw-answer storage anonymous.

## Do not build creepy data collection
- No hidden answer capture without opt-in.
- No adtech tags carrying response data.
- No employer collection by default.
- No session replay.
- No IP-derived enrichment in your database.
- No mixing optional email with answer tables.

## Do not let agent tools drift
- Do not paste a giant prompt asking an agent to fix everything.
- Do not let Claude redesign the taxonomy.
- Do not let Codex change scoring unless explicitly asked.
- Do not run overlapping agents on the same files at the same time.
- Commit after each working step.
