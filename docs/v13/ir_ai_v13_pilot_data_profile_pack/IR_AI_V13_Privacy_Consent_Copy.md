# V13 privacy and consent copy patch

Use as product copy, not legal advice.

## Result-page opt-in block

### Heading
Help improve the inventory

### Body
You can use this product without contributing your answers to the research dataset.

If you opt in, your answers and derived results will be stored in a pseudonymous research dataset to help improve the questions, test the model, and write aggregate insight posts about how people reason across IR and AI governance.

This data is not used for ads, political targeting, or sale to third parties. It is not tied to your name unless you separately volunteer contact information.

### Checkboxes
- [ ] Store my pseudonymous answers and results for research and product improvement.
- [ ] I am open to follow-up testing. Optional email stored separately from my answers.

### Helper text
Research storage is optional and does not change your result.

## Short privacy page copy

### Heading
Privacy and data use

### Body
The IR Worldview Inventory can be used without an account. Most quiz and profile data stays on your device unless you choose to save or share a result.

During beta, you may optionally contribute your answers and derived results to a pseudonymous research dataset. This helps improve the inventory, find confusing questions, and understand aggregate patterns across Foundation, Modules, AI Governance, and Profile.

We do not use response data for advertising, political targeting, or sale to third parties. Optional contact information for follow-up testing is stored separately from quiz responses.

### Bullets
- Product use works without research opt-in.
- Research response storage is off by default.
- Stored responses are versioned by questionnaire and scoring model.
- Optional contact email is stored separately from answers.
- You can request deletion of stored research data.

## Methods add-on

Because this inventory asks about political judgment, strategic values, and governance preferences, response collection is treated conservatively. Raw answer storage is optional, first-party, and separated from contact information. The product does not use worldview responses for ads, political targeting, or sale to third parties.

## Deletion copy

### Heading
Delete stored research data

If you opted into research storage and want your stored responses deleted, use the feedback form and include your respondent ID or result/session ID if available.

If you only used the on-device local profile and did not opt into research storage, clearing local data in your browser is sufficient.

## Contact opt-in copy

### Heading
Optional follow-up

If you are willing to help test future versions, you can leave an email address. This is optional and stored separately from your quiz answers.

### Helper text
Do not enter your work email if you would rather keep your participation separate from your employer.

## Success states

### Research opt-in success
Thanks. Your response has been added to the research dataset.

### No opt-in state
No problem. Your result still works normally, and your answers were not stored in the research dataset.

### Contact opt-in success
Thanks. Your follow-up contact preference was saved separately from your answers.

### Deletion request success
Your deletion request has been received. If we can match it to stored research records, those records will be removed.
